Teamfight Tactics

A game developed by Riot Games

Teamfight Tactics is a strategic free-for-all game of attrition which features champions from League of Legends. Your goal is to outlast the enemy and become the last one standing through strengthening and growing your army.

Info about Gameplay

Teamfight Tactics is an autobattler game that is a round-based PvP game. There are 8 players per match and two individuals will fight each other. You win the game by making the best team of champions from a share pool of champions, while also giving them powerful items. This has many concepts such as team building, economy management, positioning, strategy, and shared resources. There are two types of rounds in TFT, those are PvP rounds and PvE rounds. PvP rounds are when you fight a player with your army and in PvE rounds you fight monsters that can drop component items to make complete items that are better and have special effects. There are also traits that all champions possess, they get synergies by having a certain number of champions with the same traits. You can get a better team by creating a team that has contains champions with good synergy.

Little Legends, Arenas, Passes, and More

There are many items that you can buy but is not necessary for it is merely for cosmentics

Little Legends: Little Legends would be your avatar in the game, they are the ones who control the army of champions throughout the game. There are many different little legends and variants. These can be gained from eggs or bought using star shards.

Arenas: The board of every player in the game is called an arena, they are also for cosmetics purposes only.

Passes: Every set has its battlepass, battlepasses has levels that can be gained just by playing the game itself. Each level has a corresponding reward

Star Shard: Star Shards are used to buy or upgrade your little legend and they can be gained through the battlepass or store

The current TFT Set is in 6.5 and it is called Gadgets & Gizmos. The number goes up for every new set, it started from one and now its in the 6th half of the set. The .5 in the number means that it finished half of its season and once the .5 is over a new set with new traits and champions will arrive. Every set usually lasts a few months and for every set your rank gets resetted. There are unique mechanics per set, the current mechanic of the set now would be the hextech augments. The hextech augments are modifications that is given in certain rounds of the game and it can change your whole strategy towards the game. Some of this augments give you an offensive advantage while some give you an economic advantage, and some will be trait focused. You get three hextech augments per game and they can be gained in stage 1-3,3-5, and 5-1.
