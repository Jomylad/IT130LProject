Valorant

Defy the limits

Valorant is your global competitive stage. It’s a 5v5 tac-shooter matchup to plant or defuse the Spike in a one-life-per-round, first to 13 series. More than guns and bullets, you’ll choose an Agent armed with adaptive, swift, and lethal abilities that create opportunities to let your gunplay shine.

League of Legends

Our flagship game for many years

League Of Legends is our MOBA game that has amassed over 150 MILLION players We here at Riot believe that while we grew up with games we'd by at a store up front, install, and then play until the end credits rolled,then wait until the next massive expansion or till the next years iteration dropped, we don't feel that that pattern was the way to go with League. We wanted and still do, want an experience that will grow and change within a matter of weeks at a time.

Team Fight Tactics

Good tactics can save even the worst strategy

Teamfight Tactics is an autobattler game that is a round-based PvP game. There are 8 players per match and two individuals will fight each other. You win the game by making the best team of champions from a share pool of champions, while also giving them powerful items. This has many concepts such as team building, economy management, positioning, strategy, and shared resources

Hextech Mayhem

In this fast-paced rhythm runner, every action has an explosive reaction and no amount of mayhem is too much. Take on the role of yordle and Hexplosives expert Ziggs as you rampage through the neighborhoods of Piltover.
