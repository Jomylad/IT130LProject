League Of Legends is our MOBA game that has amassed over 150 MILLION players

We here at Riot believe that while we grew up with games we'd by at a store up front, install, and then play until the end credits rolled,

The Game Itself

For those unaware, League Of Legends (or LoL for short) is our Multiplayer Online Battle Arena video game that is both developed and published

in house, with help from our studios accross the globe.

The main game takes place on a 5 vs 5 map known as Sumoner's Rift, where each player takes control of a champion,

a unique character with all their own abilities and skills, as well a backstories and lore.

These champions are taken into a battle of strategy and skill, where the first team to take out the enemies nexus wins.

To achieve this, there are numerous enemy and ally turrets that either protect or attack you, as well as a jungle full of

monsters and animals to help you on your way to level up and accumlate the strength to take the enemy down.


Our Business

With LoL being one of the biggest games in the world, there is surefire profit within its veins, in fact, from our 15 million users

in 2011, in a matter of 5 years, the user base shot up to over 100 million.

With this large user base that only grows more over time, in 2021 alone we earned over 1.5 billion dollars in revenue, easily staking

us in the top ten of the most popular free-to-play games currently on the market.
