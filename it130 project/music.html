Music

Being that our fanbase has grown so much, the influence of other forms of media other than video games

was sure to fall into our laps eventually...

K/DA


K.DA was our first foray into the more mainstream music scene, we had middling success with our main

game soundtrack and extras, however with the rise in popularity of K-Pop not only as a genre but as a culture as well,

we saw it fit to bring some of our characters into the limelight.

The Group

Our main iteration of K/DA features four champions that already exist in the main universe of the game, however,

for this we gave them all new skins and a new story to come along with them.

Our four girls are Ahri, Evelynn, Kai'Sa, and Akali.

In terms of music, each character is voiced by a different person, with K-Pop girl group (G)I-dle and american Singer

Madison Beer, all voicing and singing for the group.
